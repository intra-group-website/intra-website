Jurisdictions

Approved across
every UAE jurisdiction.

DIFC Liquidation

Intra Group is an approved liquidator with the Dubai International Financial Centre. We handle DIFC company liquidations end-to-end — from filing through to final dissolution and deregistration.

UAE Mainland

Registered bankruptcy experts with Dubai Courts and UAE Federal Courts. We act as court-appointed trustees in bankruptcy and insolvency proceedings under UAE Federal Law No. 9 of 2016.

Free Zone Liquidation

Approved liquidators in DAFZA (Dubai Airport Free Zone), Jebel Ali Free Zone and Dubai Silicon Oasis. We manage the full free zone de-registration and winding-up process.

Court-Appointed Trustee

Appointed by UAE courts to serve as independent trustees in bankruptcy proceedings, creditor arrangements, inheritance disputes and complex corporate restructurings.

Creditor Advisory

We advise creditors on recovery strategies, represent creditor interests in insolvency proceedings, and negotiate with debtors on restructuring plans that maximise recovery.

Financial Distress Advisory

Early-stage advisory for directors and shareholders facing financial distress — before matters reach the courts. We help structure an orderly response and preserve value where possible.
