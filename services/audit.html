What We Do

Audit work that
stands up to scrutiny.

Our audit and assurance practice has been serving UAE businesses since 1995. We conduct external and internal audits across a wide range of industries — providing the rigorous, independent opinion that shareholders, banks, and regulatory bodies require.

Every audit engagement at Intra Group is led by a qualified CPA with direct experience in UAE commercial law and IFRS reporting standards. We are thorough, independent and precise — and our reports reflect that.

Beyond standard audit, we also advise on AML compliance frameworks, VAT implementation and corporate tax readiness — the regulatory dimensions of financial management that UAE businesses now face as a matter of course.
