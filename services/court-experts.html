What We Do

Expert opinions
built for the courtroom.

Intra Group's court experts and arbitrators have been registered with UAE Courts, DIFC and the UAE's major arbitration bodies since the early years of these frameworks. Our principals have provided expert opinion to Dubai Courts, UAE Federal Courts and the DIFC Courts in hundreds of matters across accounting, banking and real estate.

An expert witness opinion is only as credible as the expert delivering it. Courts and arbitration panels look for experts who are not only technically qualified but also registered, experienced and independent. We are all three.

We also work with law firms, corporate counsel and individuals preparing for proceedings — providing pre-filing analysis, reviewing opposing expert reports, and advising on the financial dimensions of legal strategy.
