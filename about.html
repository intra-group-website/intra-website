Our Story

One firm.
Every discipline.

Founded in Dubai in 1995, Intra Group was built on a simple conviction: that the UAE's most consequential financial and legal matters deserve a firm that can handle every dimension — not just one slice of the problem.

In the years since, we have assembled a team of certified public accountants, court-registered experts, registered arbiters and real estate professionals. Our work spans audits and financial statements, DIFC liquidations, expert witness testimony, arbitration, and property management — disciplines that most firms treat as entirely separate practices.

We don't. Our chairman and associates bring qualifications across external and internal audit, management accounting, risk management, financial restructuring, and corporate governance. When a matter touches multiple areas — as complex matters always do — our clients deal with one team, not four different firms.

We are a firm that accepts the challenges others prefer to avoid.
