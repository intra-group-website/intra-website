About Intra Group

30 Years of Trust & Excellence

With 30 years of experience since 1995, our Chairman and all our staff have distinguished qualifications and extensive experience in External/Internal Audit, Management Accounting, Risk Management, Financial Restructuring, and Corporate Governance.

The efforts of all our associates are directly focused on creating a trust-based relationship with every client — because every client is important to the group.
